The Saqra Monotourbillon - presented in 2007

TIMEPIECE CATALOGUE

deLaCour, having established the tradition of presenting their models with dual functions, have broken with custom and given birth to the Saqra MonoTourbillon.

This piece is the fruit of the collaboration between its designer Pierre Koukjian and a dedicated team of specialists from the watch making industry.

The tourbillon movement is a creation which requires the highest precision in creating this fascinating mechanism.

deLaCour offers at a glance, a thrilling view of the inner workings of the piece through the centre of the skeleton dial.
