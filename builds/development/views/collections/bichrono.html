The Bichrono

BICHRONO CATALOGUE

The Bichrono is the first timepiece released by deLaCour and sets the tone for future lines. An innovative dual time-zone watch powered by two independent chronograph movements set in the stunning oversized tonneau shaped case. The original Bichrono timepiece comes with a titanium dial and two automatic caliber DC241 movements. They have a power reserve of 37 hours and houses 33 jewels. Beating at 28.800 vibrations per hour, the movement features blued rotors, engraved balances.

The Bichrono was nominated in the best design category by the Geneva Watch making Grand Prix upon the brands unveiling in 2003. A year later it won a Unique Design Watch of the year award in Tokyo.

Not only a pioneer in its novel use of dual chronographs, the materials used to produce the different dials has been a product of constant research and innovation. World premiers, such natural precious wood (The Bichrono Hommage), pulverized asphalt (Bichrono Asphalt) and Tobacco leaf (Bichrono Fidelidad) are among these novel materials.
