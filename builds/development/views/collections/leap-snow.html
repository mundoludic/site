The Leap S Snow

Infinitely radiant and feminine, the Leap S Snow slips into a refined winter coat. Very glamorous in its fully set versions, from the dial to the tip of its horns, the Leap S Snow was made to enchant and dazzle its owner.

The pink gold case holds a diamond set dial enhanced by pink, blue or yellow sapphires making this timepiece sparkle and shine on every occasion.

No less than 3.25 cts diamonds and approx. 1cts sapphires were carefully selected and placed skillfully on this timeless watch making it lusciously feminine.
